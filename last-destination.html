Last Destination
Poster

The Golden Gate Bridge has had over 1600 reported suicides. To memorialise them I collated a list of the deceased. Each can be placed on the illustration of the bridge to their last destination.

Thankfully today the local government has put suicide prevention measures but there are still many other bridges with similar cases.
